NYSC Experience

About Me

Hi, I'm Joshua Samuel! And I recently finished my National Service, also known as NYSC! NYSC is a mandatory one-year service for every Nigerian who has completed her Tertiary education.

My journey began by reporting at the Iseyin orientation camp located in Oyo State - the state I was posted to for my service. After camp, I resumed at my PPA - place of work - and then assigned to the Sanitation CDS group.

I hope you enjoy the beautiful photos provided by my fellow 2023 Batch A Stream 2 corpers.
